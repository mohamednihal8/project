Our Functions

HealthSense AI offers five powerful diagnostic models, each built with Random Forest algorithms for high accuracy and reliability.

Symptom-Based Prediction

Diagnoses diseases from symptoms like fever or fatigue, using multi-class classification. Provides detailed descriptions and precautions, ideal for initial health assessments.

95% Accuracy

Heart Disease Prediction

Analyzes clinical data (e.g., cholesterol, blood pressure) to predict heart disease risk. Helps users seek timely cardiological care with precise binary outcomes.

90% Accuracy

Diabetes Prediction

Evaluates factors like glucose and BMI to detect diabetes risk. Supports early lifestyle changes and medical intervention for at-risk individuals.

88% Accuracy

Lung Disease Prediction

Assesses lung cancer risk based on smoking history and symptoms like coughing. Encourages early screening for high-risk users.

92% Accuracy

Thyroid Disease Prediction

Predicts thyroid cancer recurrence using pathological data. Assists patients and doctors in monitoring post-treatment outcomes.

95% Accuracy
